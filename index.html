Our Services

Here at Everything's Grand Counselling we offer services to aid you in whatever issues or difficulties you're facing. Our experienced practitioners are adept in delivering Cognitive Behavioral therapy, Logotherapy, Couples Counselling, Mental Health Services and other alternative therapies. We take pride in offering a broad range of services to our clients and our practitioners are renowned worldwide. Check out the services offered by our practitioners and their testimonials below.

Relationship Counselling

All relationships go through phases and some of these phases are more turbulent than others. Conflict, fractures and distance can erode the bedrock of a relationship. Some relationships survive these periods and grow stronger, others survive but remain heavily scarred from the traumas of conflict while some fracture and dissolve. Whatever the outcome, a key part of working through these difficult periods is finding and facing the underlying issues and creating awareness around them. We can be the 3rd party that has a birds eye view of the situation and can shed light on things that you've both been missing in the heat of conflict or the distant cold. If you and your partner have been struggling through one of these phases and would like a non judgemental 3rd party to help you recognise the trees from the forest, please send us an enquiry and one of our experienced practitioners will be happy to help.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Life is difficult and we all struggle with our emotions from time to time. Whether the stresses of life are taking over or you're still haunted by past traumas, CBT could help you find a way to cope. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) approaches emotional difficulties from a practical, problem solving methodology. Our practitioners are experts in collaborating with clients to understand their situation, the emotions related to them and how the body responds to these emotions. CBT helps clients break the vicious cycle of thoughts effecting emotions leading to unhelpful behaviours. One of our qualified practitioner will work together with you to understand your difficulties and find more useful ways of thinking and behaving in order to cope with them. If this sounds like something you think would benefit you in your current situation please don't hesitate to get in touch and we would be delighted to help.

Mental Health Services

Physical illness is something we will happily seek help for but many of us suffer in silence when dealing with a mental illness. The pain of suffering alone and bottling it all up only exasperates the illness and prolongs the suffering. The first step on the road to recovery is seeking help. Facing mental illness alone is near impossible and definiteley unnecessary. Whatever the issue is you can rest assured our practitioners have helped others through it before. Although every case is unique and the challenges can seem complicated, our practitioners employ proven methodologies to treat illnesses and disorders to help clients recover and get back to health. If you believe you're suffering from an illness or disorder, and would like a helping hand in dealing with it then please suffer no longer than necessary and reach out to one of our practitioners.

Logotherapy and Alternatives

Logotherapy is a concept founded by the famous Viktor Frankl. It is centered around the belief that happiness and fulfillment are fleeting goals to hold but, meaning and a sense of purpose are available to everybody at all times. Our service was founded by the grandson of Viktor Frankl, who has picked up where his Grandfather left off. recognised as the best logotherapist in the world, his testimonials and his work speaks for itself. Other therapies offered are wilderness therapy, dance therapy, exercise therapy, and animal assisted therapy. If any of these sound like they would be something you think might help you or you'd like more information, please send us an enquiry.
